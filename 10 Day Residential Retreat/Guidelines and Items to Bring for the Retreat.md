## ЁЯзШ Retreat Guidelines (English) ЁЯУЬ

### Practice & Commitment тЬи

* **Duration:** Be present for the **full duration** of the retreat. Stopping halfway will prevent you from gaining the complete and intended benefit. ЁЯТп
* **Foundation (S─лla):** Adhering to the Precepts and maintaining **Morality (S─лla)** is the essential key to beginning successful meditation practice. ЁЯЩП
* **Noble Silence:** **Maintain Noble Silence** at all times. This applies both to your actions (no talking, writing, reading, watching) and your mind (minimizing mental chatter). This practice cultivates deeper mindfulness. ЁЯдл

### Guidance & Solitude ЁЯСд

* **Communication:** For any need, whether urgent or simple assistance, please reach out **only to the retreat team or the teacher**.
* **Isolation:** **Do not discuss anything with other practitioners.** To maximize your focus, always imagine you are practicing alone, with the teacher and the team as the only available resource; the rest are "ghosts." ЁЯС╗

### Schedule & Discipline тП░

* **Punctuality:** **Be on time** for all scheduled activities, including sitting, walking, and mealtimes. Your punctuality contributes significantly to the flow of the collective practice. ЁЯПГтАНтЩАя╕П
* **Waking Up:** Please set an **alarm** to ensure you wake up on time. We encourage **responsibility and self-reliance** throughout the course. ЁЯФФ

### Health & Conduct ЁЯНО

* **Hydration:** Keep a **water bottle** with you at all times to stay properly hydrated. ЁЯТз
* **Eating Principle:** **Eat less.** The key principle for better meditation is to fill your stomach to only **80%**. A full stomach often leads to drowsiness and hinders focused practice. ЁЯН╜я╕П
* **Dietary Rules:** **No outside food** is allowed. We request that you do not ask for special dietary accommodations beyond basic, essential requirements. Everyone is equal and will receive the same respect and food. ЁЯдЭ
* **Respect:** **Respect** everyone, including fellow practitioners, volunteers, and the amenities provided. Please maintain good behavior, recognizing the generosity of all who support the retreat. ЁЯзШ

### Journaling & Gear тЬНя╕П

* **Journaling:** We highly recommend maintaining a **meditation journal** using a notebook and pen. Writing offers a profound impact on one's life. Note-taking and journaling at the end of each session are great ways to track progress. ЁЯУЭ
* **Sitting Support:** You may bring your **own preferred meditation mat, cushion, or gear**. While basic support is provided, bringing your own can enhance comfort. ЁЯзШ
